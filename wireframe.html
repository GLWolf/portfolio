Description

This app will test your knowledge of the green lantern comics. It’s a 10 question quiz that use HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JQuery. This is a fun app to play with and test your knowledge. I plan on updating a few futures and adding an audio files to the quiz. This will read the questions and answers to you also give a brief history to each question.

Hurdles

Most of the hurdles I face during this project were typos and syntax errors. Coding at 3am, not the best idea. I have to come up with ways for the text and the background to get along and not make it hard to read. I have already started on the audio function and it has been challenging and fun.

What I learned

I learned a lot during my time building this app. Creating listeners that would iterate to the right question and bring up the right answers. Learning to add audio files and adding them to the app. Have not updated the master branch still working on the kinks.
